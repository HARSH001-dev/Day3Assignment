In a quiet suburban neighborhood, life was ordinary until one fateful night. The sky lit up with strange, ominous lights, signaling the beginning of an alien invasion. Chaos erupted as people scrambled for safety. Amidst the turmoil, a mother and her two children were separated from their father. Their task was clear: survive and reunite with him. The mother, determined and resourceful, led her kids through the darkened streets, avoiding alien patrols and scavenging for supplies. They found temporary refuge in an abandoned building, but the danger was far from over. The mother devised a plan to reach a rumored safe zone where survivors were gathering. They moved stealthily, using the shadows to their advantage, and fought off alien drones with makeshift weapons....
